Final Chat App HW13

  • Problem: Many people who make plans to go somewhere often have to exit out of the chat to search for the location on Google Maps. This is inconvenient and disrupts the flow of the conversation. When they find the place, they either need to screenshot and send it to the chat or they tell the friend the place and then they need to look up the location themselves. This makes it annoying to make plans or discover new places to eat, shop, or explore.
  • How my design addresses this problem: My design centers around sharing locations in the chat. If you type in the location input, there will be a dropdown menu of autocomplete suggestions from Google Maps. This ensures that you are searching for the intended location and in the correct format. When you send the location, an interactive embedded maps of the location will be displayed in the chat. You can scroll, zoom in, and explore the area around the location. I accomplished this with the Google Maps autocomplete API and the Google Maps Embed API. Additionally, the location sent in the chat is also a clickable link that takes you directly to the place on Google Maps. There is also a Maps icon next to the location input that takes you to Google Maps for a standard search. Finally, there is a clickable list of the recent locations mentioned in the chat. This makes finding places extremely easy and displays everything in the chat with additional options to take you directly to Google Maps.
